# 🌱 FAO Plant Genetic Resources Learning Portfolio

## About
Welcome! 

This repository documents my learning journey through the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) e-learning programme on Plant Genetic Resources for Food and Agriculture (PGRFA).

As a Plant Science and Biotechnology graduate, these courses broadened my understanding of plant genetic resource conservation, genebank management, global information systems, and digital tools used to support crop improvement and food security.

More importantly, they shifted my research interests toward computational agriculture, where biological knowledge is combined with data science, artificial intelligence, and digital technologies to improve plant breeding and agricultural sustainability.

---

## Courses Completed
- ✅ **Introduction to Plant Genetic Resources for Food and Agriculture**
- ✅ **GRIN-Global Community Edition (GGCE) and Genesys**
- ✅ **Global Crop Conservation Strategies**
- ✅ **Global Information System (GLIS)**

Rather than simply completing online courses, I wanted to organize my learning into a public knowledge portfolio that demonstrates my growing interest in computational agriculture, plant breeding, and agricultural data systems.
