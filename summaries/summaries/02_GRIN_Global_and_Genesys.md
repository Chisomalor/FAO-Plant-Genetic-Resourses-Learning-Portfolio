# GRIN Global and Genesys

## Course Overview

This course introduced two important global information systems used to document and share plant genetic resources: GRIN Global and Genesys. These platforms help researchers, gene banks, and plant breeders manage, preserve, and access valuable germplasm information worldwide.

## Key Concepts Learned

- GRIN Global is a gene bank information management system used to document and manage plant genetic resources.
- Genesys is an international portal that provides access to millions of plant genetic resource accessions conserved in gene banks around the world.
- Accurate documentation improves the conservation, exchange, and utilization of plant genetic resources.
- Digital databases support collaboration among researchers and institutions globally.
- Standardized data management enhances the accessibility and long-term preservation of genetic resources.
